Bvlgari Unveils New High Jewelry Collection, Aeterna

Jewelry Maison has unveiled its most extravagant collection to date, Aeterna, in celebration of its 140th anniversary in Rome. The line consists of over 500 masterpieces, including high jewelry, watches, bags and fragrances.

Drawing from the timeless beauty of Rome, the Aeterna High Jewellery collection is a tribute to Bvlgari's 140-year-long vocation to perpetual reinvention. Born from the Maison’s ability to embrace the spirit of each era, it is a journey through the ever-evolving eclectic creativity and aesthetic codes of the Roman High Jeweller.

More than 140 masterpieces open the doors to a universe of boundless vision and eternal splendour. The world's most precious gems, grown in the depths of the Earth over millions of years, find pride of place into inspired designs, in a perpetual creative reinvention that transcends time. And as every dawn brings a new light, every creation holds a fragment of eternity in the endless cycle of time.

A jubilation of precious colours brought to life through Bvlgari’s signature smooth cabochon cut. Inspired by the Lotus flower, a symbol of eternal rebirth across cultures, the Bvlgari Lotus Cabochon necklace combines a wealth of gemstones bursting with life and energy into a work of art that echoes Haute Couture mastery. Breathing new life into Bvlgari’s archives, its design stems from an original synthesis of iconic heritage creations reinvented in an unprecedented form.
